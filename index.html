Self Introduction

I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Informatics, School of Multidisciplinary Sciences, SOKENDAI (The Graduate University for Advanced Studies), as a member of programming research laboratory in National Institute of Informatics (NII) in Japan. My supervisor is Prof. Zhenjiang Hu.

My email is chunmiaoli1993 AT gmail DOT com or chunmiaoli1993 AT nii DOT ac DOT jp.
My address is Programming Research Lab (Room 1611), National Institute of Informatics, 2-1-2 Hitotsubashi, Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo, 101-8430, Japan.

I finished my bachelor degree from Northwest University in 2015 (with rank 1/167 and GPA 3.74/4) and got the master degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (advisor: Prof. Yuxi Fu) in March, 2018.

You can find my detailed CV here .

Research and Publication

  • Chunmiao Li, Yijun Yu and Zhenjiang Hu. Collaborative Development Environment: Fusing Human Insights into Automated Program Repair.   • Chunmiao Li and Xiaojuan Cai. Hardness Results for Coverability Problem of Well-Structured Pushdown Systems. In Proceedings of the 11th International Conference on Language and Automata Theory and Applications (LATA 2017). LNCS 10168, pp 435-446, 2017. [PDF]
    Previous oral presentation at the 2nd Young Researchers Workshop on Formal Methods YR-SETTA 2016

  • Chunmiao Li, Xiaojuan Cai and Guoqiang Li. Lower Bound for Coverability Problem of Well-Structured Pushdown Systems. Journal of Software, 2018 [PDF]

  • Chunmiao Li and Xiaojuan Cai. On Hardness of Recursive Integer Program Analysis. In the 1st National Conference on Formal Methods and Applications (FMAC’16), 2016, accepted (as poster)

My current research interest focuses on programming languages, especially in bidirectional transformations (BXs), which aim to build the consistency on two different things that enjoy the same contents. I'm trying to apply BXs to promote the data sharing in blockchian. Before, I did some researches on pushdown systems.

More about me

I was born and grew up in China, in a small town named Shangluo. My favourite food is Baozi, a local delicious breakfast in my hometown. Noodles is still my staple food even I'm struggling to adapt rice now.

I love to listen to music and play the piano. I often sing some songs with musics accompanied.
